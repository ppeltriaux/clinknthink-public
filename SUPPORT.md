## About

**Clink & Think** is a gentle marble-and-tubes puzzle game for young children
(ages ~4–6). Drop glossy marbles into glass tubes, match the card, sort by colour,
spot patterns, mix colours, and play through a friendly Adventure map — learning
counting and colours along the way. No ads, no tracking, works offline.

**Requirements:** iPhone or iPad running iOS 17 or later.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is it free?
Yes — Clink & Think is free to download and play. Free Play, the Learn mode, the
Pattern mode, and the first Adventure world (the Playroom) are all free. A single
optional one-time **Full Unlock** ($2.99) opens every mode, all Adventure worlds,
all difficulties, and lets you jump straight to any mode.

### How do I unlock the marble themes?
Themes (fruit, animals, dinosaurs, space, and more) are **earned by playing** —
clear an Adventure world to unlock its marbles everywhere. Paying parents who'd
rather not wait can flip the parent-gated **"Unlock all themes"** switch in
Settings.

### How do I restore a purchase on a new device?
Open the store screen and tap **Restore Purchases**, signed in with the same Apple
ID you bought it with. Your Full Unlock will be restored at no extra charge.

### Why is there a question before the store / settings?
That's the **parental gate** — a quick puzzle so a child can't make a purchase or
change important settings by accident. Adults can answer it in a second.

### My progress didn't carry over to my other device.
Progress syncs through **iCloud**. Make sure both devices are signed in to the same
Apple ID with iCloud enabled (iOS Settings → your name → iCloud), and that you're
connected to the internet. Sync happens automatically in the background.

### How do I reset everything?
In **Settings → Reset progress**. This clears local progress and the iCloud copy
(stars, stickers, earned themes, and your decorated Playroom).

### What languages does the voice support?
The spoken numbers, colours, and encouragement are available in **English,
French, Spanish, Portuguese (Brazil), Italian, and German**. By default the app
follows your device language; you can also pick one in Settings.

### Is my child's data safe?
Yes. The app collects **no personal data** of any kind. See the
[Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).
