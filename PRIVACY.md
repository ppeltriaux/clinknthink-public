# Privacy Policy — Clink & Think

**Effective date:** 17 June 2026
**Applies to:** Clink & Think (iPhone & iPad), version 1.0 and later
**Developer:** Pascal Peltriaux

## The short version

**Clink & Think does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information — from anyone, including children.** There are no user accounts, no
sign-in, no advertising, and no third-party analytics or tracking. The game works
fully offline.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts,
photos, microphone, or any other personal data, and it contains no advertising or
analytics SDKs.

## Information stored on your device

The game saves your progress locally on your device — things like which Adventure
levels you've cleared, stars and stickers earned, unlocked marble themes, and your
settings (sound, music, voice language). This data never leaves your device except
through Apple's iCloud, as described below. It is removed if you delete the app or
use the in-app **Reset progress** option.

## iCloud sync

If you are signed in to iCloud and have iCloud enabled, your game progress and
settings sync automatically across your own Apple devices using Apple's **iCloud
Key-Value Storage**. This data is stored in your private iCloud account, governed
by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). The developer
has no access to it and cannot read it. You can turn this off in iOS Settings →
your name → iCloud, and you can clear synced data with the in-app **Reset
progress** option.

## Purchases

Clink & Think is free to download and play. It offers one optional **one-time
"Full Unlock"** in-app purchase. All purchases are handled entirely by **Apple**
through the App Store — the developer never receives or sees your payment
information. Purchases (and the Settings that affect them) are placed behind a
**parental gate** so a child cannot buy anything without an adult.

## Speech

Some modes speak words aloud (numbers, colours, encouragement) using Apple's
on-device speech synthesis. **No audio is recorded, and the microphone is never
used.**

## Children's privacy

Clink & Think is designed for the App Store **Kids Category** and is suitable for
young children (ages ~4–6). Because the app collects **no personal information at
all**, it does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or under 16 in
the EEA/UK). It complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
(COPPA) and the EU/UK GDPR by design — there is simply no data collection.

## Third parties

The app uses **no** third-party SDKs, ad networks, or analytics providers. The only
external service involved is Apple itself (iCloud and the App Store), which you
already use as part of iOS.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective
date. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.
